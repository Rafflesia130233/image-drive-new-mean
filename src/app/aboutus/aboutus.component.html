Who we are?

We sell ‘Hobby items’. During this period, most of us wanted to have a hobby to stay out of all the research stuff. So, some started singing songs and then recording them. Some started painting, sewing, and knitting. Every weekend we build one of these products alternatively. Now, some don’t have any place in room to keep those. And as they have created those with care, they are not willing to destroy those. We believe there exist so many people who have a hobby but are not willing to make those their main professions. So we buy and sell ‘Hobby items’
